Rita Borsellino
||||||
Rita Borsellino (Italian pronunciation: [ˈriːta borselˈliːno]; 2 June 1945 – 15 August 2018) was an Italian Sicilian anti-Mafia activist, politician and, between 2009 and 2014, a Member of the European Parliament (MEP) for the Democratic Party.
