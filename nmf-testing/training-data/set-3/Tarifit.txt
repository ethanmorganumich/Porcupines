Tarifit
||||||
Tmazight or Tarifit Berber, also known as Riffian (Tarifit: Tmaziɣt [θmæzɪχt]) is a Zenati Berber language spoken in the Rif region in northern Morocco. It is spoken natively by some 1,271,000 Rifians primarily in the Rif provinces of Al Hoceima, Nador, Driouch, and as a minority language in Oujda, Tetouan and Berkane.
