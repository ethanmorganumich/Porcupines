Takestan
||||||
Siadehan or Siaden (Tati: سیادن, Siyâden), also known as Takestan (Persian: تاكستان Tâkestân), is a city and capital of Takestan County, in Qazvin Province, Iran. At the 2011 census its population was 120,907. Takestan has a railway station on the Teheran-Tabriz line.  The name Takestan literally means "vineyard".
