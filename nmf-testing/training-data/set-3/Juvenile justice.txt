Juvenile Justice (TV series)
||||||
Juvenile Justice (Korean: 소년 심판) is a 2022 South Korean streaming television series. Directed by Hong Jong-chan, it stars Kim Hye-soo, Kim Mu-yeol, and Lee Sung-min. The series, which tells the story of a judge who is known for her dislike of juveniles and gets appointed as judge of a juvenile court, was released on Netflix on February 25, 2022.
