Italian charm bracelet
||||||
An Italian charm bracelet also known in America as a nomination bracelet  is a series of individual modular links hooked together on a stretchy band to form a single charm bracelet. A typical Italian charm bracelet comprises eighteen Italian charm links. Each link features an Italian charm face (design or image) soldered onto the actual Italian charm link.
