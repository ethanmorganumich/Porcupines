Durumagi
||||||
Durumagi (Korean: 두루마기; lit. closed all around), also jumagui (Korean: 주막의; Hanja: 周莫衣), juchaui (Korean: 주차의; Hanja: 周遮衣), juui (Korean: 주의; Hanja: 周衣), is a variety of po, or overcoat, in hanbok, the Korean traditional garment. It is a form of outwear which is usually worn as the topmost layer of clothing; that is it worn over jeogori (jacket) and baji (pants).: 120
