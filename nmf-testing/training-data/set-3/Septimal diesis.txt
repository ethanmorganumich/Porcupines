Septimal diesis
||||||
In music, septimal diesis (or slendro diesis) is an interval with the ratio of 49:48 play , which is the difference between the septimal whole tone and the septimal minor third.  It is about 35.7 cents wide, which is narrower than a quarter-tone but wider than the septimal comma. It may also be the ratio 36:35, or 48.77 cents. Play
