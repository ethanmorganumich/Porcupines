Yukaghir people
||||||
The Yukaghirs, or Yukagirs (Northern Yukaghir: одул, деткиль (odul, detkil), Russian: юкаги́ры) are a Siberian ethnic group people in the Russian Far East, living in the basin of the Kolyma River.
