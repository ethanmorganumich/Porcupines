NetEase Cloud Music
||||||
NetEase Cloud Music (Chinese: 网易云音乐) is a freemium music streaming service developed and owned by NetEase, Inc. The streaming service was launched to the public on 23 April 2013. As of April 2017, the platform has 300 million users, and a music database consisting of over 10 million songs.  In April 2017 it received its series A financing of 750 million CNY (US$107 million), and was valued at 8 billion CNY (US$1.14 billion), making it a unicorn company. It is one of the biggest competitors in the Chinese music streaming business, primarily competing with Tencent's QQ Music.
