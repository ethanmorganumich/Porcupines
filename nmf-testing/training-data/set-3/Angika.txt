Angika
||||||
Angika (also known as Anga, Angikar or Chhika-Chhiki) is an Eastern Indo-Aryan language spoken in some parts of the Indian states of Bihar and Jharkhand, as well as in parts of Nepal.It is closely related to languages such as Maithili, Bengali, Bhojpuri and Magahi.
