Polish American Congress
||||||
The Polish American Congress (PAC) is an American umbrella organization of Polish-Americans and Polish-American organizations.
Its membership has fraternal, educational, veterans, religious, cultural, social, business, political organizations, and individuals.
As of January 2009, it lists 20 national organizations as members.
It is subdivided into 41 divisions and chapters.Traditionally, the PAC National President has also been the president of the largest Polish-American fraternal organization, the Polish National Alliance (PNA).
