Savi Savi Nenapu
||||||
Savi Savi Nenapu (Kannada: ಸವಿ ಸವಿ ನೆನಪು) (transl. Sweet memories) is a 2007 Indian Kannada film starring actor Prem and Mallika Kapoor. The title of the film is based on a song from My Autograph (2005).
