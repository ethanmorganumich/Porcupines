Mandaloun (horse)
||||||
Mandaloun (foaled March 18, 2018) is a retired American Thoroughbred racehorse who won the 2021 Kentucky Derby and Haskell Stakes. He also won the 2021 Risen Star Stakes and Pegasus Stakes, and the 2022 Louisiana Stakes.
