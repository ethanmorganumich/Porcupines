White voice
||||||
White voice (Ukrainian: білий голос, romanized: bilyi holos, Polish: biały głos) or white singing (Ukrainian: білий спів, romanized: bilyi spiv, Polish: biały śpiew) is a contemporary name, used usually in Eastern Europe, for the traditional singing style. Alternative names for the traditional folk technique of singing are: open voice, full voice, natural voice.
