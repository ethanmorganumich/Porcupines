Yahoo! Maktoob
||||||
Maktoob (Arabic: مكتوب) was an online services company founded in Amman (Jordan). Maktoob.com was known for being the first Arabic/English email service provider. In 2009, Yahoo! acquired Maktoob.com, thus turning Maktoob into Yahoo!'s official arm in the MENA region. As of October 28th, 2022 Yahoo Maktoob no longer publishing content.
