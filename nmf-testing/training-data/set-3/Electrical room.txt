Electrical room
||||||
An electrical room is a room or space in a building dedicated to electrical equipment. Its size is usually proportional to the size of the building; large buildings may have a main electrical room and subsidiary electrical rooms. Electrical equipment may be for power distribution equipment, or for communications equipment.Electrical rooms typically house the following equipment:

Electric switchboards
Distribution boards
Circuit breakers and disconnects
Motor control centers
Transformers
Busbars
Electricity meters
Backup batteries in a Battery room
Fire alarm control panels
Distribution framesIn large building complexes, the primary electrical room may house an indoor electrical substation.
