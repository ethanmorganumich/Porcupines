Jumpstyle
||||||
Jumpstyle is an electronic dance style and music genre popular in Western Europe, originally in Belgium.
Jumpstyling is often referred to as "Jumpen": a combination of the English word 'Jump' and the Dutch and German suffix '-en' (meaning "to jump", or "jumping").It originated in Belgium but gathered bigger popularity in their neighbouring country the Netherlands in the 2000s.
