Gavi (footballer)
||||||
Pablo Martín Páez Gavira (Spanish pronunciation: [ˈpa.βlo maɾˈtiŋ ˈpa.eθ ɣaˈβiɾa]; born 5 August 2004), known as Gavi (pronounced [ˈɡaβi]), is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder for La Liga club Barcelona and the Spain national team.
