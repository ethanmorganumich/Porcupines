Kumaoni people
||||||
Kumaonis, also known as  Kumaiye and Kumain (in Nepal), are an Indo-Aryan ethno-linguistic group who speak Kumaoni language as their first-language and live mostly in Kumaon region in the Indian Himalayas and parts of the Sudurpashchim Province in far western Nepal.
Kumaoni is also used as an  address for people who have their origin in Kumaon. The word Kumain is a direct derivative of Kumaoni.
