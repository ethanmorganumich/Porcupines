Five-point electoral law
||||||
Five-point electoral law, of five-adjectives election is a concept used in Polish political science referring to the elections that are:

universal
direct
equal
proportional
anonymous (secret ballot).A similar concept is used in elections to the Israeli Knesset, with a sixth adjective, "national (No constituencies)", added, and "general" used instead of "universal".
