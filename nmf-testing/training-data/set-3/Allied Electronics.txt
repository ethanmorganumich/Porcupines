Allied Electronics
||||||
Allied Electronics & Automation is a United States based authorized distributor of industrial automation products, electronic components and electromechanical products. Allied is a subsidiary of RS Group, and it is an ISO 9001:2008+AS9120A certified company with 46 sales offices serving the United States, Canada, Mexico and Chile.
