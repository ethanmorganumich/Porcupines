Haryanvi language
||||||
Haryanvi (हरियाणवी hariyāṇvī or हरयाणवी harayāṇvī) 
is an Indo-Aryan language spoken in the state of Haryana in India, and to a lesser extent in Delhi. Haryanvi is considered to be part of the dialect group of  Western Hindi, which also includes Khariboli and Braj. It is written in the Devanagari script.
