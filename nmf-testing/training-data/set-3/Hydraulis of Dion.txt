Hydraulis of Dion
||||||
The Hydraulis of Dion Greek: Ύδραυλις του Δίου; romanized: Ídravlis tou Díou) is a unique exhibit of the Archaeological Museum of Dion. It is the earliest archeological example of a water organ to date.
