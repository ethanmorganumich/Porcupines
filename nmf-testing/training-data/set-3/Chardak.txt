Chardak
||||||
A chardak (Bulgarian, Macedonian and Serbian: чардак, čardak)  is an old typical house in the Balkans. It is derived from the word cardak, which is a component of Ottoman Turkish house design. This term, which is also called sofa, denotes an open hall of a house's upper living floor.
