Sindhi cap
||||||
The Sindhi cap, also known as the Sindhī ṭopī (Sindhi: سنڌي ٽوپي) rarely known as the Sindhi Kufi (Sindhi: سنڌي ڪفي), is a skullcap worn predominantly by Sindhis in Sindh, Pakistan. Together with Ajrak or Saraiki Ajrak, the Sindhi cap is regarded as an essential part of Sindhi Saraiki culture, and Balochi culture.
