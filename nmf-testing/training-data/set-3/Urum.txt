Urums
||||||
The Urums, singular Urum (, ; Greek: Ουρούμ, Urúm; Turkish and Crimean Tatar: Urum, IPA: [uˈɾum]) are several groups of Turkic-speaking Greek Orthodox people in Crimea and Georgia.
