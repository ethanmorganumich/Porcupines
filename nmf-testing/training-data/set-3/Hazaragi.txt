Hazaragi dialect
||||||
Hazaragi (Persian: هزارگی, romanized: Həzārəgī; Hazaragi: آزرگی, romanized: Āzərəgī) is an eastern dialect of Persian that is spoken by the Hazara people, primarily in the Hazarajat region of central Afghanistan, as well as other Hazara-populated areas of Afghanistan. It is also spoken by the Hazaras of Pakistan and Iran and also by the Hazara diaspora living elsewhere in the world.
