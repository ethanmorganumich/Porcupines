Grameen check
||||||
Grameen check (Bengali: গ্রামীণ চেক gramin chek "rural plaid") is a type of clothing design in Bangladesh. It mainly constitutes a pattern of squares or rectangles formed from different colors of dyed threads.  Clothes with grameen check designs are mainly made of cotton fabric woven in the traditional methods by the cottage-industry workers in Bangladesh.
