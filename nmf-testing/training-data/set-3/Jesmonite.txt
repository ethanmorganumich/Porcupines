Jesmonite
||||||
Jesmonite is a composite material used in fine arts, crafts, and construction.  It consists of a gypsum-based material in an acrylic resin. It was invented in the United Kingdom in 1984 by Peter Hawkins, as of July 2019 still the technical and development director of the Jesmonite company.
