Kusunda language
||||||
Kusunda or Kusanda (endonym Mihaq Kusunda: [miɦjɑːk] (listen)) is a language isolate spoken by a few among the Kusunda people in western and central Nepal. As of 2022, it only has a single fluent speaker, although there are efforts underway to keep the language alive.
