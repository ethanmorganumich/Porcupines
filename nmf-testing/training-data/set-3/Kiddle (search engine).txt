Kiddle (search engine)
||||||
Kiddle.co is a web search engine and online encyclopedia emphasizing safety for children. Kiddle search is powered by Google Programmable Search Engine and employs SafeSearch with additional filters. Kiddle is powered by Google Custom Search but is not affiliated with Google LLC. However, it has been mistaken for a Google product in several news articles and blogs due to the nature of its name.
