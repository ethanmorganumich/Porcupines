Warp knitting
||||||
Warp knitting is defined as a loop-forming process in which the yarn is fed into the knitting zone, paraeele to the fabric selvege. they form vertical loops in one course and then move diagonally to the next course to knit the next course. Thus the yarns zigzag from side to side along the length of the fabric. Each stitch in a course is made by many different yarns. Each stitch in one wale is made by several different yarns.
