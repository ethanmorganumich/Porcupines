Outline of law
||||||
The following outline is provided as an overview of and topical guide to law:Law (article link) is the set of rules and principles (laws) by which a society is governed, through enforcement by governmental authorities.  Law is also the field that concerns the creation and administration of laws, and includes any and all legal systems.
