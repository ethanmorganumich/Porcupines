Septimal diatonic semitone
||||||
In music, a septimal diatonic semitone (or major diatonic semitone) is the interval 15:14 Play .  It is about 119.44 cents. The septimal diatonic semitone may be derived from the harmonic series as the interval between the fourteenth and fifteenth harmonics (B7b and B).
The septimal diatonic semitone equals a just diatonic semitone Play  (16:15, or 111.73 cents) plus a septimal kleisma (the interval 225:224, or 7.71 cents).
