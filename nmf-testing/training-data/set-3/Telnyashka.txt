Telnyashka
||||||
The Russian telnyashka (Russian: тельняшка, IPA: [tʲɪlʲˈnʲaʂkə]) is an undershirt horizontally striped in white and blue (occasionally black or green) and which may be sleeveless. It is an iconic uniform garment worn by various Russian military formations.
