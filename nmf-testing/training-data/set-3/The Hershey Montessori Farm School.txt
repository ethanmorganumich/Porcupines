Huntsburg Township, Geauga County, Ohio
||||||
Huntsburg Township is one of the sixteen townships of Geauga County, Ohio, United States. As of the 2010 census the population was 3,645, up from 3,297 at the 2000 census.Huntsburg is home to a sizable Amish community that is part of the Middlefield settlement, the fourth largest in the world.
