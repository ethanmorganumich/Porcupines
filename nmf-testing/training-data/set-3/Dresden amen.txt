Dresden amen
||||||
The Dresden amen (Dresdner Amen) is a sequence of six notes sung by choirs during church services in the German state of Saxony since the beginning of the 19th century. The motif was first used in, and is particularly associated with, the city of Dresden.
The sequence has been used in various forms by composers since the 19th century.
