Kelaghayi
||||||
Kelaghayi (Azerbaijani: Kəlağayı), also known as "chārqad" (çarğət from Persian چارقد), is a traditional Azerbaijani women's headgear. It is a square-shaped silk head scarf with special prints on it. In November 2014 at the 9th session of UNESCO's traditional art and symbolism of Kelaghayi, its production and the wearing were included in the list of intangible cultural heritage UNESCO.
